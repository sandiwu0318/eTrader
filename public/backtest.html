BB (Bollinger Bands)


Middle line is SMA. Upper and Lower Bands are 2 standard deviations away from the middle.


RSI (relative strength index)


The magnitude of recent price changes to evaluate overbought or oversold.


SMA (Simple Moving Average)


Average price over a specified period.


WMA (Weighted Moving Average)


Like SMA but applies more weight to data that is more current.


EMA (Exponential Moving Average)


Like WMA but applies exponentially.
